About ME

Here is a little more about me!

My name is Camille, a student passionate about marketing, specifically in the Sports Industry. I love learning, creating, and finding ways to connect people through meaningful experiences.

Ive had the chance to work in a couple of different roles, but my Marketing internship with Wisconsin Athletics has helped me understand my passion for sports. Being able to adpat quickly, work as a team, and be able to make a fans day has been rewarding.

I am driven by the things that matter most to me: faith, family, friends, and my career. They keep me grounded, give me purpose, and push me to keep growing. Staying true to those not only has allowed me to show up in those areas of life, but they have also shaped me into the woman I am today.

Outside of my professional and academic life, I enjoy being outdoors, going to sporting events, long walks, music, and dancing. I try to to incorporate the way these things make me feel into everything I do!
