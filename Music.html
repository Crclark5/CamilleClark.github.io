My All-Time Top 5 Albums

It's always hard to narrow down the favorites, but here are the albums that consistently define my taste in music. Each one is a masterpiece!

  1. Rumours by Fleetwood Mac: A pop-rock masterpiece that layers complex, emotionally raw songwriting over impeccably crafted, iconic melodies.
  2. Frontiers by Journey: An 80s rock album packed with powerful anthems and defining vocal performances by Steve Perry.
  3. Leather The Deluxe Version by Cody Johnson:An authentic and soulful modern country collection that honors traditional roots while showcasing gritty, contemporary storytelling.
  4. Dizzy Up The Girl by The Goo Goo Dolls: A definitive 90s alternative rock record famous for blending introspective ballads with high-energy guitar tracks, featuring my all time favortie song, "Iris.".
  5. Appetite for Destruction by Guns n' Roses: An iconic album that all generations know, with the redefined hard rock and its iconic guitar riffs and timeless rock-and-roll attitude.

I find that my favorites tend to lean towards soft rock, rock, and country, but I always make time for new discoveries in any genre and will listen to anything!
